This is my Story

This is a story about a boy called Charles and his journey with software.

Why I got into Tech?

I think the thing I love most about technology is how it has provided me an outlet to express myself creatively through the ideas I think of and build. I think in today's day and age technology is what makes the world go around. I love to solve problems and use my creativity and this passion for technology has been the driving force for my continued learning in the field.

How I have been learning?

Since I graduated from Brunel University with a Bachelor's in Computer Science, I've actually spent some time playing professional basketball, but that hasn't slowed down my passion for tech. During this time, I taught myself how to build websites and have started my own freelance side hustle (acquahxweb.com). Investing time and money into resources such as Leetcode, CodeAcademy, Mimo, freeCodeCamp has been great for my growth and these platforms have become my best friends over the years. My real life friends who work in tech have also been of great assistance to me. I feel as though a supportive team around you is always important while learning too. I was also blessed enough to take part in a Coding Skills Bootcamp at the end of last year provided by Rubik Talent and receive a certificate for this. This has furthered my desire to deepen my understanding of software development. Expanding my knowledge of JavaScript and Frameworks like React has allowed me to explore my creativity through personal projects I have built on my GitHub.

My Goal

My ultimate goal is to one day become a senior developer and eventually use my skills to set up a tech academy in my parents home country on Ghana. Technology has shaped my life and provided me with so many opportunities to learn and better myself. I would like it to do the same for people where access to certain resources is not so common.
